1

Install Driver

Download and install the ViGEmBus Driver on your PC. This tells Windows that a real controller is plugged in.

2

Run Server

Launch XboxController Server.exe on your PC. It will display a QR code and a local IP address (like 192.168.1.100:5000).

3

Connect App

Open the app on your phone (or go to the IP address in your browser). Enter the IP shown on your PC, and you're ready to play!
